    Design Process

    Communicating Vision and Validating Idea via Contextual Inquiry

    We discussed about some common problems which students and faculy face during their day to day life.

    Creating Affinity Diagram and Analyzing it

    We wrote down the user reviews on colored sticky notes and then clustered them into groups depending on their similarity. We gave titles to these clusters.

    Formalizing the Idea.

    Based on the discussions among team members, we made the rough layouts of the interface.

    Rapid Prototyping

    We created prototypes of our app in paper and gave some users for providing the feedback.

    Writing Code

    We started with the coding part of the application in this last phase, putting our ideas and design into a real life application.

    About the project

    The project was done as part of Designing Human Centered Systems course at IIIT Delhi, under the guidance of Dr. Ponnurangam Kumaraguru ("PK") . The idea behind Interacto was to ease the process of interaction between the faculty and students, schedule meetings within a few taps, help students to find a suitable project topic as per their area of interests and at the same time assist faculty members in finding sincere and hardworking students.
    Interacto is a student-faculty interaction app which:

    1. It provides UI to find free slots common for all attendees.
    2. Helps in scheduling meetings within a few taps
    3. Notifies the user (Faculty and Students) about the upcoming lectures and meetings.
    4. Shows the consolidated list of project opening to students, so that they may get a chance to explore new problems as per their area of interest.
